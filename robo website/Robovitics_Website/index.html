Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private deemed university founded by G. Viswanathan in 1984. Its mission is to improve students’ lives through excellence in education and research. The Department of Student Welfare plays an irreplaceable role in creating a lively, fun, and resourceful community for students to employ their momentous years. Every year they join hands with VIT’S Clubs and Chapters to make every student find an environment where they can learn and grow together.

We are a group of ardent tech enthusiasts with the zeal to learn, to build and a thirst to be the best. A small group of passionate techies to explore different domains of technology has now blossomed into the official Robotics club of VIT, Vellore. Our mission is to spread knowledge about robotics by organizing various free workshops and seminars that help people out in finding their technical niches and build their own projects. Our members collaborate on some incredible projects and nurture glorious teams that represent us all over the world, and have several accolades to their name. With pride, all RoboVITians try to live up to the legacy left by our predecessors.

EVENT

EQUINOX

Equinox, hosted by RoboVITics, is an official Major League Hacking member hackathon. Equinox offer its participants, from all around the world, a platform to express their grit and zeal to create something inspiring and bring their imagination to life, all from the comfort of their homes. They are given 36 hours and the intellectual freedom to select the track and problem statement they see fit and come up with avant-garde solutions. Equinox not only rewards its participants with exposure and experience, but also has exciting goodies, prizes, and plenty of schwag to win. All of this is bundled with resources, mini-workshops and mentors to guide the hackers innovate and create the next big breakthrough.

VORTEX360

Vortex 360 is an Autodesk-powered online design hackathon organized by RoboVITics. This hackathon gives the participants a creative platform to portray a whirlwind of design ideas. They are encouraged to traverse the depths of their minds to solve the given problem statements that range over six fields. All-inclusive, this hack provides the participants with the playground to transcend creativity, learn and compete, resulting in an unparalleled experience.

ROBOWARS

As the flagship event of gravitas, ROBOWARS is a combination of a plethora of engaging one-of-it's-kind workshops and enthralling battles. Experience the true mayhem of one of India's biggest combat robotics event where teams from around the globe compete in various weight categories initiating three days of ultimate robot fighting for the title. Held in the largest and safest arena in India, ROBOWARS is a thrilling phenomenon that you absolutely cannot miss.

HOR

At the altar of Ideation and Innovation, Technical inadequacy should never be an impediment. We at RoboVITics believe in inculcating Skills that help each and every person with an idea and give them a shot at materialising something out of thin air. The Hands-On-Robotics Workshop is our brainchild that aims to initiate the inquisitive minds on the path of Robotics and to give them a glimpse into its future prospects. A variety of activities including but not limited to building bots of different types using IR, ultrasonic Sensors and to top it all off a final show off amongst participants for the claim of the "Best Bot".

MACHINE DESIGN

Empowered with AutoDesk's latest user-friendly software, Fusion 360, participants get a chance to interact with the extraordinary world of CAD design and discover their love for it. Guided with CAD experts, students gain industrial level experience. They get to bring their imagination to life and turn their worldview 360 degrees with their brilliant designs. Machine Design is the right platform to learn something new and design something great.

COSMOS 360

COSMOS 360 is a design hackathon where budding CAD designers get to showcase their talents using Fusion360. In this 24-hour hackathon, participants get to imbibe their curiosity and intellect into designs and come up with something concrete, somewhat dynamic and simply cosmic. It is the place for participants to polish their design abilities and compete with like-minded people inspired to design the universe and beyond.

KARTAVYA

We, at RoboVITics, believe it is our kartavya to give back to society and make a difference in the lives of the underprivileged. RoboVITics has collaborated with educational institutions across India to spark curiosity in young minds.

We introduce children to valuable STEM skills which helps them to build a better future for themselves. Our sessions not only involve fun activities but also focus on bringing out their untapped potential. Bringing smiles to their faces and learning from their visionary minds is an absolute delight for us :)
